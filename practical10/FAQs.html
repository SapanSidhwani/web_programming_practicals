Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Lamborghini logo represent?

The Lamborghini logo features a charging bull, which is a reference to the founder, Ferruccio Lamborghini's, zodiac sign, Taurus.

Is Lamborghini owned by another company?

Lamborghini is owned by the Volkswagen Group, a German automotive conglomerate that also owns brands like Audi, Porsche, and Bugatti.

How many Lamborghinis are produced each year?

Lamborghini produces a limited number of cars each year, with production numbers varying by model. In 2020, Lamborghini produced 7,430 cars, which was a record year for the company.
