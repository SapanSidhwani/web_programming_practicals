Lamborghini

Lamborghini is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer that was founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini. The company is headquartered in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy and is currently owned by the Volkswagen Group through its subsidiary Audi. Lamborghini is known for its high-performance and exotic sports cars that are designed with sleek and aerodynamic lines, powerful engines, and luxurious interiors. The company's models include the Huracán, Aventador, and Urus SUV. Lamborghini also produces limited edition and special edition models, such as the Sian and Centenario, that are designed with unique features and advanced technologies.
